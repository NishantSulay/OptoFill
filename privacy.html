Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 1, 2025  ·  Last updated: July 1, 2025

Short version: OptoFill does not collect, store, or sell any personal data. Your API key stays on your device. Dictations are sent only to Anthropic's API to process your request and are not retained by this extension.

1. Who We Are

OptoFill ("we", "our", "the extension") is a Chrome browser extension that helps optometrists and eye care professionals document patient exams faster using voice dictation and AI. OptoFill is developed and maintained by OptoFill.

Contact: privacy@optofill.com

2. What Data OptoFill Handles

a) Anthropic API Key

  • You provide your own Anthropic API key in the extension's Settings panel.
  • This key is stored locally on your device only using Chrome's chrome.storage.local API.
  • It is never transmitted to OptoFill servers. It is sent only to api.anthropic.com as part of the AI request you initiate.

b) Voice Dictations / Transcripts

  • Speech is transcribed on-device using the browser's built-in Web Speech API (Google Chrome's speech recognition). OptoFill does not receive or store audio.
  • The resulting text transcript is sent to api.anthropic.com when you click "Fill Fields." This is the only external transmission of transcript data.
  • OptoFill does not store, log, or retain any transcript content on its own servers.

c) Form Field Metadata

  • When you click "Fill Fields," OptoFill scans the visible form fields on your active browser tab (labels, field names, input types). This metadata is included in the Anthropic API request to help Claude identify which field to fill.
  • This data is not stored by OptoFill and is not transmitted to any service other than Anthropic.

d) Custom Instructions

  • Any custom instructions you enter in Settings are stored locally via chrome.storage.local and sent to Anthropic's API as part of the system prompt when you process a transcript.

3. What OptoFill Does NOT Do

  • Does not operate any servers or databases that store your data.
  • Does not collect analytics, usage statistics, or telemetry.
  • Does not track which websites you visit.
  • Does not share any information with third parties other than Anthropic (solely for AI processing at your explicit request).
  • Does not sell, rent, or monetize user data in any form.
  • Does not store or transmit audio recordings.

4. Third-Party Services

Anthropic (Claude AI)
When you click "Fill Fields," a request is sent to api.anthropic.com containing your transcript and form field metadata. This request is governed by Anthropic's Privacy Policy. Anthropic may process this data according to their API terms. We recommend reviewing their policy if you process sensitive patient data.

Google Chrome Web Speech API
Voice transcription is performed by Chrome's built-in speech recognition. Audio may be processed by Google's servers depending on your Chrome configuration. Refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details.

5. HIPAA Considerations

OptoFill is a software tool, not a covered entity or business associate under HIPAA. If you use OptoFill to process Protected Health Information (PHI):

  • You are responsible for ensuring your use complies with HIPAA and applicable regulations in your jurisdiction.
  • We recommend avoiding inclusion of patient names, dates of birth, or other direct identifiers in your dictations where possible — clinical findings (sphere, cylinder, IOP values) are sufficient for form filling.
  • Anthropic offers a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) for eligible API customers. If PHI processing is required, obtain a BAA with Anthropic before using OptoFill in a clinical setting.

6. Data Retention

OptoFill retains no data on its own. Data stored locally (API key, custom instructions, session transcript) persists only in your browser's local storage and can be cleared at any time by:

  • Clearing your Chrome extension storage (Settings → clear API key)
  • Removing the OptoFill extension from Chrome
  • Clearing Chrome's site data for the extension

7. Permissions Explained

  • activeTab — Allows OptoFill to interact with the tab you are currently viewing when you click the extension icon. Required to read and fill form fields on your EHR page.
  • storage — Allows local storage of your API key and settings on your device only.
  • scripting — Allows OptoFill to inject the field-scanning script into your active EHR tab when you trigger a fill. No scripts run without your explicit action.
  • host_permissions (<all_urls>) — OptoFill must work on any web-based EHR system. Because different optometry practices use different platforms at different URLs, the extension cannot pre-specify a fixed list of domains. This permission is used only when you actively click "Fill Fields."

8. Children's Privacy

OptoFill is intended for licensed eye care professionals. It is not directed at or designed for use by individuals under the age of 18.
