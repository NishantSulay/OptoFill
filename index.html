How It Works

From spoken words to completed charts in seconds

OptoFill works silently in the background on any EHR or optometry platform — no integration required.

🔌
1

Install the Extension

Add OptoFill to Chrome in one click. It works on any web-based EHR or optometry platform without any IT setup.

🎙
2

Activate Voice Mode

Click the OptoFill mic icon or press Ctrl+Shift+O. The extension begins listening as you speak naturally with your patient.

🧠
3

AI Processes Speech

Our clinical AI understands optometry-specific terminology — prescriptions, IOP readings, visual acuity, and more — in real time.

⚡
4

Fields Auto-Fill

OptoFill maps extracted data to the correct form fields on your screen and populates them instantly and accurately.

✅
5

Review & Confirm

A summary panel highlights every filled field. Edit anything with a click, then approve to finalize the record.

Features

Everything you need to document faster

Built specifically for optometrists and ophthalmologists who want to spend less time on paperwork.

🎙

Real-Time Voice Dictation

Speak naturally during the exam. OptoFill transcribes and extracts clinical data without interrupting your workflow.

🧠

Optometry-Aware AI

Trained on clinical optometry language including sphere, cylinder, axis, prism, IOP, and visual acuity notations.

🌐

Works on Any EHR

Compatible with WebPT, Eyefinity, Crystal PM, and any web-based practice management system — zero integration required.

📋

Smart Field Mapping

OptoFill automatically detects form fields on the page and maps extracted values to the right input every time.

🔒

HIPAA-Compliant

Audio is processed locally and never stored. All transmitted data is encrypted end-to-end to keep patient records private.

✏️

One-Click Corrections

Instantly edit any auto-filled field from the OptoFill sidebar before confirming. Corrections train the AI for next time.
